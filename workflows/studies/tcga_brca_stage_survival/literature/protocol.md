# Qualification evidence rationale

`NAS-BRCA-001` reproduces an established clinical association to qualify the
platform; it is not an evidence-synthesis or product-claim study. The detailed
association, endpoint, model, limitations, and expected direction are locked in
the analysis plan. No claim from this qualification study may be presented as
clinical validation or decision support.

Future discovery studies require a formal, review-approved literature protocol
and reproducible search strategy before retrieval begins.
