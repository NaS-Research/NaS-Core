# Acceptable Use Policy

Researchers must use data only for the purpose recorded in its active source
registration and the approved research protocol.

Prohibited activity includes:

- attempting to identify or contact research participants;
- combining sources to defeat privacy protections;
- using a source after suspension, expiration, or deletion;
- sharing credentials or access tokens;
- copying source data into Git, prompts, issues, logs, or unapproved systems;
- publishing restricted source content;
- bypassing access, AI, export, or publication decisions; and
- representing exploratory or model-generated text as verified evidence.

Suspected misuse requires immediate suspension of the affected source and an
incident review.
