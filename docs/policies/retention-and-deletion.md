# Retention and Deletion Policy

Data is retained only for the documented research and contractual period.

- Every licensed source requires a retention period or an explicit documented
  reason that the agreement imposes none.
- Review dates are monitored separately from retention dates.
- Expired authorization suspends new processing immediately.
- Deletion covers object storage, databases, indexes, caches, working copies,
  and provider-side material where applicable.
- Backups follow their separately documented expiration schedule.
- Deletion produces an auditable record containing scope, time, operator, and
  outcome without retaining deleted sensitive content.

Research releases may retain permitted manifests, provenance, and aggregated
results after raw data deletion only when the source terms allow it.
